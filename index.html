About Us

Established since 1985, El Gaucho was the first restaurant in Sotogrande to open to the public. This house favourite steakhouse is a benchmark in the Costa del Sol. First of its kind in the area, it has catered to our family and friends over 40 years and counting. Our vision is simple, a family run restaurant, that offers the highest quality steaks.
